InterfaceBase¶

This is the stub of most interface-structures, meaning they extend on in.

For example Mainscreen.GetBounds or Chatbox.GetBounds() those methods are inherited from here.

TInterfaceBase¶

type
  TInterfaceBase = record
    FName: String;
    FBounds: TBox;
    FStatic: Boolean;
  end;
